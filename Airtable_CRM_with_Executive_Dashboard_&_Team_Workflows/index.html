üóÇ CRM Structure

Leads Management
A dedicated Leads table tracks incoming prospects with standardized sources and statuses.
Filtered views highlight new leads, qualified leads, and leads requiring follow-up.

Client & Project Management
Clients are linked to converted leads and associated projects.
Each project includes type, budget, dates, status, and related tasks, allowing managers to assess progress and risks.

Task Tracking
Tasks are connected to projects and assigned to team members with priorities, due dates, and status tracking.
Smart views help the team focus on high-priority tasks and upcoming deadlines.

Financial Tracking
A Finance table tracks revenue and expenses per project.
Formulas calculate profit, enabling monthly performance review and forecasting.
