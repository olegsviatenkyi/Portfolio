This project was developed for a mortgage broker and real estate agent who needed to automate post-meeting tasks and client communication.

Using Make.com, I built an automated workflow that activates right after the client finishes a Zoom call.
The system automatically retrieves the recording, extracts the audio file, and sends it to OpenAI for transcription.
Once the transcript is ready, OpenAI analyzes the conversation to identify key discussion points and next steps for both the broker and the client.

Based on these insights, OpenAI generates a draft follow-up email, summarizing the meeting results and suggested actions.
All extracted data ‚Äî meeting notes, highlights, and email drafts ‚Äî are automatically saved to Airtable CRM.

To improve flexibility, a review and rewrite system was implemented in Airtable:
if the broker wants to adjust the follow-up email, they can provide feedback directly in Airtable, and the AI will regenerate the message accordingly.
