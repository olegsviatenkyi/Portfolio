This project demonstrates an automation that updates contact data in Google Sheets based on email engagement events.

The goal was to automatically capture and synchronize contact information when a recipient clicks a specific link in an email. The system checks whether the contact already exists in Google Sheets and updates their data, or creates a new record if the contact is not found.

The solution was designed to remove manual data handling and ensure accurate, up-to-date contact records connected directly to marketing activity.

The automation was built using Make.com as the core integration platform, connecting ActiveCampaign and Google Sheets.

Workflow logic:

  1. ActiveCampaign triggers the automation when a contact clicks a specific email link

  3. Google Sheets is searched for an existing contact
