üìå Project Overview

This project focused on building an automated contact management workflow that synchronizes email engagement data with Google Sheets.

The automation tracks when a contact clicks a specific link in an email campaign and automatically updates their information in a central Google Sheets database. If the contact already exists, their record is updated. If not, a new contact entry is created.

The solution was implemented using Zapier and lightweight automations in ActiveCampaign, ensuring reliable and consistent data handling without manual intervention.

Manual contact tracking after email interactions often leads to outdated records, missed updates, and data inconsistencies.

The client needed a simple and dependable way to:

  • Capture contact activity triggered by email clicks
  • Keep Google Sheets data accurate and up to date
  • Avoid manual data entry and repetitive work

üõ†Ô∏è Solution Architecture

The automation workflow was designed with the following logic:

  1. Email link click trigger from ActiveCampaign
  2. Contact lookup in Google Sheets
  3. Conditional logic:
    • Update existing contact if found
    • Create a new contact if not found
  4. Data synchronization between ActiveCampaign and Google Sheets

Zapier handled the orchestration, while Google Sheets served as the single source of truth for contact data.
