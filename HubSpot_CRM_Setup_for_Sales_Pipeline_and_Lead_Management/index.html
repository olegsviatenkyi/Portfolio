HubSpot CRM Setup for Sales Pipeline and Lead Management

👤 Role: HubSpot CRM and Sales Automation Specialist

🔍 Project Overview

This project focused on building a dedicated HubSpot CRM structure for a sales team inside an existing HubSpot account. The account was already used for another department, but the sales process needed its own structure, fields, views, pipelines and reporting.

The main goal was to make lead management, company records, deal tracking and sales reporting clearer and easier to use.

🎯 Business Challenge

The client needed a clear CRM setup for managing sales opportunities, leads, companies and dealers in HubSpot.

  • No dedicated CRM structure for the sales team
  • Company, contact and deal data needed better organization
  • Lead sources and lead statuses were not clear enough
  • The deal pipeline needed clear stages and rules
  • The team needed useful views for daily sales work
  • Leadinfo data had to be connected and reviewed in HubSpot
  • Management needed dashboards for sales performance

🛠️ Solution

I built a structured HubSpot CRM setup for the sales team and organized the main CRM objects, fields, pipelines and views.

The solution included:

  • Setting up Companies, Contacts and Deals for the sales process
  • Creating and organizing custom CRM properties
  • Building a sales deal pipeline with clear stages
  • Creating lead statuses for qualification and follow-up
  • Setting up contact, company and deal views
  • Creating executive sales dashboards
  • Reconnecting Leadinfo to HubSpot
  • Enabling the needed Leadinfo sync settings
  • Mapping key Leadinfo fields into HubSpot

⚙️ CRM Structure

Company Records
Company records were structured with important business fields such as country, city, industry, employee count and cargo category.

Contact Records
Contact records were organized with lead source, lead status, role, job title and other fields needed for sales follow-up.

Deal Records
Deal records were set up with sales stage, amount, country, dealer commission fields, delivery date and lost reason.

Associations
Companies, contacts and deals were connected so the sales team could understand each opportunity in one place.

📊 Sales Pipeline and Lead Management

A sales pipeline was created to manage opportunities from early contact to closed deals. Lead statuses were also organized to help the team separate new leads, outreach, dealer leads, hot leads and planned meetings.

  • Sales pipeline stages
  • Lead qualification board
  • Dealer lead status
  • Hot lead review views
  • Lost reason tracking
  • Deal value and weighted amount visibility

🔗 Leadinfo and HubSpot Setup

Leadinfo was reconnected to HubSpot and the needed sync settings were enabled. I also configured field mapping so key Leadinfo data could be used inside HubSpot company records.

  • HubSpot integration checked and reconnected
  • Company sync settings enabled
  • Website visit logging reviewed
  • Leadinfo field mapping configured
  • Hot lead views adjusted for team review

📈 Reporting and Dashboard

I created a sales dashboard to give the team and management a clearer view of sales activity and performance.

  • Pipeline value
  • Won revenue
  • Deal activity
  • Win rate
  • Forecast reporting
  • Country-level sales insights
